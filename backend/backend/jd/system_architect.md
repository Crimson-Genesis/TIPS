## Job Description: **System Architect**

**Role Summary**
Designs, defines, and governs the overall technical architecture of systems to ensure scalability, security, reliability, and alignment with business goals.

**Key Responsibilities**

* Design end-to-end system architecture (software, hardware, network).
* Define architectural standards, patterns, and best practices.
* Translate business requirements into technical solutions.
* Make high-level technology choices (frameworks, platforms, databases).
* Ensure system scalability, performance, fault tolerance, and security.
* Review and approve technical designs from engineering teams.
* Identify and mitigate technical risks early.
* Guide development teams on architectural decisions.
* Ensure integration between subsystems and third-party services.
* Document architecture using diagrams and specifications.

**Required Skills**

* Strong knowledge of system design, distributed systems, and networking.
* Experience with cloud platforms, containers, and CI/CD pipelines.
* Understanding of security principles, authentication, and authorization.
* Ability to evaluate trade-offs between performance, cost, and complexity.
* Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.

**Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
* 7+ years of experience in software/system engineering.
* Prior experience designing large-scale systems.
