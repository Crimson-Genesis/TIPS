## Job Description: **Product Manager**

**Role Summary**
Owns the product vision and roadmap, translating customer needs and business objectives into actionable requirements for engineering and design teams.

**Key Responsibilities**

* Define product vision, strategy, and roadmap.
* Gather and prioritize product requirements.
* Write clear product requirement documents (PRDs).
* Work closely with engineering, design, and stakeholders.
* Balance business goals, user needs, and technical constraints.
* Define success metrics and track product performance.
* Conduct market research and competitive analysis.
* Manage product backlog and prioritize features.
* Coordinate product launches and iterations.
* Act as the primary decision-maker for product scope.

**Required Skills**

* Strong understanding of software development lifecycle.
* Ability to translate business problems into product features.
* Excellent communication and stakeholder management.
* Data-driven decision making.
* User-centric thinking and prioritization skills.

**Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Business, or related field.
* 3–6+ years of product management experience.
* Experience working with agile/scrum teams.
